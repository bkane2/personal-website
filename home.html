Bio

I'm an aspiring Researcher & Developer in the field of Artificial Intelligence. I'm currently an undergraduate student (class of 2019) pursuing a BSc degree in Computer Science and a BA degree in Economics at the University of Rochester. My current research interests and experience lie in knowledge representation & commonsense reasoning, natural language processing, dialogue planning, and the applications of these to the social sciences.

I have a particular fascination with the history and future of human development, and how the relationships between labor and technology have changed over time. Having participated in large-scale creative tasks with other humans, I'm motivated by the possibility of enabling human-computer collaboration on similarly large-scale tasks, allowing us to automate many menial processes and greatly improve the human condition. My ultimate goal is, first and foremost, to help develop technology which frees minds and betters lives.
